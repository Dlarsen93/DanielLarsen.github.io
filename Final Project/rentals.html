Welcome to Cozumal

Welcome to the island of Cozumal just off the coast of Mexico. Here we have beautiful beaches, a wonderful sea breeze, and stunning views of the ocean all around the island.

At ~Scoots we have everything you need to get around the island enjoys all the beauty it has to offer. With two convientant locations: Playa del Carmen and the Terminal puerta Maya. We offer a variety of rentals for everyone from honey-mooners and families.
